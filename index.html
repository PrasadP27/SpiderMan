history

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in August 1962 . Spider-Man is the alter ego of Peter Parker, an orphaned high school student who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Since then, Spider-Man has become one of the most popular and commercially successful superheroes worldwide

villains

Dr. Octopus

Doctor Octopus is a supervillain in the Marvel Comics universe and is one of Spider-Mans most iconic foes. He is a brilliant scientist who uses his mechanical tentacles to commit crimes and take over the world

Lizard

A scientist researching genetics, he injected himself with an experimental serum made from reptile DNA which transformed him into a humanoid lizard. As the Lizard, he has regeneration abilities, along with superhuman strength, speed, and agility.

sandman

Once a small-time crook, he became a supervillain after his body merged with sand which he can manipulate in many ways, such as shapeshifting, increasing his density and strength to lift up to 85 tons, and creating dust storms from his body.

Green Goblin

The first Green Goblin, Norman Osborn, is the CEO of Oscorp and has powers derived from a "Goblin formula" that increases intelligence, agility, endurance, strength, and reflexes to superhuman levels. Thus formula causing insanity as a side effect.

Venom

Eddie Brock is a former reporter who blamed Spider-Man for ruining this career and his life. He became Venom after binding with the symbiote that once merged with Spider-Man. As Venom, he has the same powers as Spider-Man, and aims to ruin his life in any way he can.
